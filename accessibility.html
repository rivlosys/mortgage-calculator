Accessibility Commitment

Rivlosys is committed to ensuring that financial tools are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Key features of our accessibility strategy include:

  • High-contrast text and interactive elements.
  • Keyboard-navigable calculators and menus.
  • Screen reader compatible labels and ARIA attributes.

If you encounter any barriers on our site, please contact us at helpinbx@gmail.com.
