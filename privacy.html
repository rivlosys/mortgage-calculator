Privacy Policy

Transparency and privacy are our core values. Rivlosys does not store, collect, or sell any financial information entered into our calculators.

  • All calculations are performed locally in your browser (Client-side).
  • We use Umami Analytics (privacy-first, cookieless) to track anonymous usage patterns and improve the tool.
  • The "Share" feature generates a dynamic URL containing your inputs for your own use; this data never touches our database.
