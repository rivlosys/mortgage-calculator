Analyze the full cost of carrying a $600,000 mortgage in the 2026 Canadian market.

How much is a $600,000 mortgage per month?

For a $600,000 home with a minimum down payment, your monthly payment will typically range from $3,400 to $3,900 depending on your interest rate and amortization period.

What to Consider

At $600,000, your minimum down payment is $35,000 (5% on first $500k, 10% on next $100k). This puts you in the "high-ratio" category, meaning CMHC insurance will be added to your mortgage balance.
